About

This application uses a fine-tuned BERT model to classify text into four sentiment categories:

  • Positive: Expresses favorable opinions or emotions
  • Negative: Expresses unfavorable opinions or emotions
  • Neutral: Objective or balanced statements
  • Irrelevant: Off-topic or unrelated content
